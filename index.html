Mastering the Basics

1. Placement

Usually: Intensifier + Adjective

"It is very cold." / "She is extremely smart."

2. The "Enough" Rule

Enough comes after the word it modifies.

"The water is warm enough." (NOT enough warm)

3. Too vs. Very

Too usually means there is a problem (negative).

"It's too hot to drink." (I can't drink it)

4. Extreme Adjectives

Use absolutely or totally for extreme words.

"It is absolutely freezing." (NOT very freezing)
